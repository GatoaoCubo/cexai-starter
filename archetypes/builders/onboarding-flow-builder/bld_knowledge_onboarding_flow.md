## Domain Overview  
User onboarding flows are critical for converting new users into active, engaged product users. Activation milestones define sequential steps that guide users toward value realization, while aha-moment design focuses on creating emotional or functional breakthroughs that trigger retention. Effective flows balance simplicity with clarity, leveraging behavioral science principles to reduce friction and accelerate time-to-value. Industries like fintech, SaaS, and gaming prioritize onboarding as a competitive differentiator, often using data-driven activation funnels to optimize retention rates.

## Common Patterns  
1. **Progressive Disclosure** – Reveal features incrementally based on user progress.  
2. **Micro-Interactions** – Use subtle animations to reinforce user actions.  
3. **Aha Moment Triggers** – Embed value-clarifying moments at key milestones.  
4. **Gamification** – Reward completion of activation steps with badges or unlocks.  
5. **Just-in-Time Help** – Provide contextual guidance during critical tasks.  
6. **Feedback Loops** – Use immediate confirmation (e.g., success states) to reinforce behavior.  

## Pitfalls  
- Overloading users with too many steps or information.  
- Skipping aha-moment design, leading to disengagement.  
- Inconsistent feedback that confuses user progress.  
- Ignoring contextual cues (e.g., device, user role).  
- Failing to align onboarding with long-term product goals.
