## Crew Role  
Integrates govtech solutions tailored to specific verticals (e.g., healthcare, education), ensuring alignment with policy goals, technical feasibility, and stakeholder needs. Coordinates cross-functional teams to deliver scalable, compliant systems.  

## Receives From  
| Builder       | What                  | Format         |  
|---------------|-----------------------|----------------|  
| Policy Analyst| Vertical-specific mandates | Policy briefs |  
| Legal Review  | Compliance guidelines  | Legal memos    |  
| Tech Architect| System architecture specs | Tech diagrams |  

## Produces For  
| Builder       | What                  | Format         |  
|---------------|-----------------------|----------------|  
| Implementation Team | Deployable system blueprints | Architecture docs |  
| Compliance Officer | Vertical-specific audit trails | Compliance reports |  
| Stakeholders  | Use case demonstrations | Demo scripts   |  

## Boundary  
Does NOT handle cross-vertical policy harmonization (legal_vertical) or audit-driven compliance checks (compliance_checklist). These are managed by dedicated legal and audit builders.
