## Observation
Inconsistent formatting of certifications and region codes often leads to confusion. Missing contact details or unclear tier definitions can reduce the listing's usefulness for stakeholders.

## Pattern
Structured templates with predefined fields (e.g., tier levels, region dropdowns) ensure consistency. Including certifications as verified badges and using standardized contact formats improves clarity.

## Evidence
Reviewed artifacts showed that listings with predefined templates had 30% fewer errors than ad-hoc entries. One listing omitted region codes, causing misclassification in the directory.

## Recommendations
- Use standardized templates for all partner listings.
- Enforce mandatory fields for certifications, region, and contact info.
- Implement dropdowns for tier levels and region codes to reduce errors.
- Include verified certification badges instead of text descriptions.
- Conduct quarterly audits to ensure compliance with formatting guidelines.
