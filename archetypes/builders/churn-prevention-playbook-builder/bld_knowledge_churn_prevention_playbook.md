## Domain Overview
Churn prevention is critical in SaaS, telecom, and subscription-based industries, where retaining existing customers often yields higher ROI than acquiring new ones. Playbooks focus on detecting early signals of dissatisfaction, triggering timely interventions, and deploying scripted strategies to re-engage at-risk accounts. Key metrics include churn rate, customer lifetime value (CLV), and net promoter score (NPS), with emphasis on predictive analytics and behavioral data.

Playbooks integrate customer success (CS) workflows, leveraging tools like CRM systems, AI-driven analytics, and automated communication platforms. Success hinges on aligning signal detection (e.g., usage drops, support escalations) with intervention triggers (e.g., personalized outreach, discount offers) and scripted actions (e.g., account health reviews, executive sponsorships).

## Industry Standards
- Gainsight CS Maturity Model: health score design, CTA automation, playbook execution
- ChurnZero Playbook Framework: risk score segmentation and automated outreach sequences
- Totango SuccessBLOCs: structured intervention playbook design and outcome tracking
- Bain & Company Customer Loyalty Practice: NPS-to-retention signal integration
- TSIA Customer Success Research: save-the-account script patterns and win-back cadences
- "Predictive Analytics for Churn" (Eric Siegel, 2015): leading-indicator signal selection

## Common Patterns
1. Proactive outreach based on usage pattern anomalies
2. Tiered intervention triggers (e.g., low, medium, high risk)
3. Personalized retention scripts with dynamic variables
4. Automated alert systems for real-time signal detection
5. Cross-functional collaboration between CS and marketing

## Pitfalls
- Over-reliance on historical data without real-time signal detection
- Ignoring qualitative feedback (e.g., support tickets, surveys)
- Misaligned KPIs between sales, CS, and product teams
- Over-automation without human judgment in high-risk cases
- Failing to segment accounts by churn risk or value
