## Crew Role  
Strategizes and executes growth initiatives for existing customers, identifying upsell/cross-sell opportunities, creating tailored solutions, and coordinating with product/eng teams to scale adoption.  

## Receives From  
| Builder       | What                          | Format         |  
|---------------|-------------------------------|----------------|  
| Customer Success | Customer feedback/usage data  | Document       |  
| Product       | Feature roadmap updates       | Email          |  
| Sales         | Existing account health       | Dashboard      |  

## Produces For  
| Builder       | What                          | Format         |  
|---------------|-------------------------------|----------------|  
| Sales         | Expansion play strategies     | Playbook       |  
| Product       | Feature prioritization requests | Jira ticket  |  
| Leadership    | Expansion ROI metrics         | Dashboard      |  

## Boundary  
Does NOT handle churn prevention (churn_prevention_playbook) or new customer acquisition (sales_playbook). Does NOT manage technical implementation; Engineering handles deployment.
