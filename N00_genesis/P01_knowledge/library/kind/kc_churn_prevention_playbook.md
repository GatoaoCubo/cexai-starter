# Churn Prevention Playbook

## Signal Detection
- Decreased engagement (e.g., login frequency, activity)
- Negative feedback (reviews, support tickets)
- Payment issues (failed payments, account suspension)
- Inactivity (no logins for X days)
- Churn indicators (account closure, unsubscribe)

## Intervention Triggers
- Personalized outreach (email, phone call)
- Offer discounts or promotions
- Provide onboarding or training
- Address specific concerns (e.g., technical issues)
- Re-engage with loyalty programs

## Save-the-Account Scripts
- **Email Script**: "We noticed you've been less active. Can we help?"
- **Phone Script**: "We value your business. Let's discuss how we can improve your experience."
- **Follow-up Email**: "Thank you for your feedback. Here's how we'll address your concerns."

## Continuous Monitoring
Regularly review metrics and adjust strategies based on feedback and data.
