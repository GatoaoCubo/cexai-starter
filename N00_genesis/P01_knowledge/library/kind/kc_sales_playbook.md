# Sales Playbook

## Personas
- Primary: Decision Maker (Budget authority)
- Secondary: Technical Lead (Requirements validator)
- Stakeholder: End User (Feature adopter)

## Discovery Flow
1. Initial Outreach (Cold/Warm)
2. Needs Assessment (Problem identification)
3. Solution Alignment (Value proposition)
4. Budget Discussion (Pricing negotiation)
5. Timeline Agreement (Implementation schedule)

## Objection Handling
- **Price Concerns**: "We offer tiered pricing models"
- **Feature Limitations**: "Our platform scales with your needs"
- **Implementation Risk**: "We provide 90-day free trial"
- **Competitor Comparison**: "Our solution has 3x faster onboarding"

## Close Patterns
- **Commitment Close**: "Let's schedule your onboarding"
- **Alternative Close**: "Would you prefer a demo first?"
- **Trial Close**: "Start with a 30-day free trial"
- **Negotiation Close**: "We can adjust the terms to fit your needs"

## How to use

```text
ROLE: you are a sales agent (or the N06 nucleus) running a structured deal.
1. Identify the persona in play (Decision Maker / Technical Lead / End User) and tailor framing.
2. Walk the Discovery Flow in order; do not skip Needs Assessment before pricing.
3. Match each objection to its Objection Handling response; keep claims truthful to the offer.
4. Select a Close Pattern that fits the buyer's signal (commitment / alternative / trial / negotiation).
Primary 8F verb: INJECT (this playbook is consumed at F3 to ground sales copy and dialogue).
```
