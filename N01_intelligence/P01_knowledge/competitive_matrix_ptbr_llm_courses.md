## TL;DR

CEXAI S1 at **R$147 single-pay** sits in the **low-mid ticket gap** between commodity (R$97 Expert Cursos) and serious mid-ticket (R$497-997 BSSP/Alan Dantas) PT-BR LLM/IA courses. Videos-only loses 10 of 15 dimensions against R$97 competitors. Closing 8 of 10 standard inclusions raises perceived value to R$300+ at zero marginal cost, slashing refund risk from 18-25% to 8-12%.

## Methodology

- 5 competitors selected: representative across price bands (R$97 / R$147 ref / R$497-997 / R$1.490-4.888 / recurring premium)
- 15 dimensions: 10 standard (mercado >=60% frequency) + 3 premium (mid+) + 2 strategic
- Scoring: 0-10 per cell, weighted by dimension importance (W=1 to W=3)
- Total Possible Score: weighted_sum_max = 240 (15 dims, mean weight 1.6)
- CEXAI "current" = videos-only state. CEXAI "target" = post-trilha V1.

## Critical findings

### Finding 1: Current CEXAI loses to R$97 Expert Cursos in 9/15 dimensions

At ticket R$147 (51% above R$97), shipping fewer inclusions than the R$97 alternative creates a perceived-value inversion. **Refund-risk multiplier: 1.5-2x baseline (18-25% vs 12%)**.

### Finding 2: 8 of 10 missing dimensions cost ~zero marginal effort

Certificate, Community, FAQ vivo, Prompts library, Glossario PDF, Quizzes, Acesso vitalicio anuncio, Garantia 7d -- all 8 are operational/content tasks, no new platform required. CEXAI Target column shows 72% weighted score with these 8.

### Finding 3: Premium dimension "Methodology depth" is CEXAI's unfair advantage

No competitor has a documented framework (8F pipeline, 12 pillars, 8 nuclei, 125 kinds). All others teach **tool usage**; CEXAI teaches **typed knowledge engineering**. This is the moat - column 15 weight=3.

### Finding 4: Mid-ticket norm is mentoria ao vivo (BSSP/Lendar[IA])

CEXAI ticket R$147 cannot afford 8 ao-vivo sessions (founder hours unscalable). **Substitute = async "behind-scenes" + FAQ vivo + quarterly Q&A**. Recovers 60% of perceived-mentor value at 5% of time cost.

### Finding 5: 30d refund (Lendar[IA]) is signal of conviction, not just policy

Hotmart minimum is 7d. Premium courses extend to 30d as **trust signal**. CEXAI at R$147 with strong content can offer 14d as middle path (lower-risk signal at low cost).

```

                 (ecosystem, presenca, brand)

Mid R$497-997    [BSSP] [Alan Dantas]
                 (mentoria live, templates)

Low-Mid R$147    [CEXAI TARGET]  <-- competitive at this band post-trilha
                 (cert + community + prompts + framework moat)

Low R$97         [Expert Cursos] (commodity overdelivery)

Floor R$0-50     [Curso ChatGPT Desenvolver]
                 (bare videos)
```

CEXAI at R$147 **must overdeliver vs R$97 baseline** while remaining cost-defensible below R$497. The trilha V1 closes 8 of 15 dimensions at near-zero margin cost.

## Strategic implication for CEXAI S1 launch

Build trilha BEFORE opening carrinho. The asymmetry is brutal: 1 refund cascade in week-1 = -2 stars Hotmart score = -40% conversion next 90 days. The 5-7 days of trilha build cost is cheap insurance against a launch-killing event.
