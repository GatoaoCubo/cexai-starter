## TL;DR

The Brazilian infoproduto market is **R$10.6B in 2025**, projected R$100B+ by 2026, with average ticket R$155 and 12% baseline refund rate. CEXAI S1 at R$147 sits **at the median ticket** but **below the average inclusion bundle**, exposing a 2x refund-risk multiplier. Three case studies (Expert Cursos R$97, BSSP R$997, Lendar[IA] R$4.888) define the price-vs-inclusion frontier. CEXAI must close 8 standard inclusions to honor R$147 and reach 5-8% refund (best-tier).

## Market Size (TAM/SAM/SOM)

### TAM -- Brazilian Infoproduto (all categories)

- **2025 size**: R$10.6 billion (Jornal do Bras / Accio research, 6.5% CAGR from R$8.8B 2023 baseline)
- **2026 projection**: R$100B+ (broader digital products + services projection)
- **Consumers**: 20+ million Brazilians purchasing infoproducts
- **Avg ticket**: R$155 across all infoproducts (cursos online, ebooks, webinars, mentorias)
- **Avg purchases/year**: 2.8 per consumer

### SAM -- LLM/IA infoproduto segment (PT-BR)

- Estimated **3-5% of TAM** (LLM/IA boom 2024-2026): **R$320-530M annual** (2025)
- Growth rate **20-30% YoY** (faster than overall TAM driven by ChatGPT/AI adoption)
- Top platforms: Hotmart (dominant), Eduzz, Kiwify, HeroSpark
- Active SKUs estimate: **8.000-12.000** PT-BR IA courses on Hotmart alone (heavy long-tail)

### SOM -- CEXAI realistic capture (S1, 12 months)

| Scenario | Customers Y1 | Revenue Y1 | Notes |

| Pessimistic | 100 | R$14.700 | Videos-only, refund 25% |

**SOM realistic 12mo**: 500-2.000 customers = **R$73.500-294.000 GMV before Hotmart fee 9.9% + R$1**. Net to founder: ~R$66k-265k.

### The R$147 trap

R$147 is **perception-arbitrage zone**:
- Above R$97 floor -> buyer expects "more than" the R$97 option
- Below R$197-297 mid -> seller cannot afford live mentoria, in-person events
- Result: must overdeliver on **inclusions** (quasi-free items: cert, community, prompts, glossary, quizzes, FAQ vivo, behind-scenes, manifesto ebook)

## Case Studies (3 named with URLs)

### Case A: Expert Cursos -- "R$97 Overdelivery Floor"

- **URL**: https://experttcursos.com.br/inteligenciaartificial
- **Price**: R$97 (de R$297), 12x R$10.03
- **Inclusions**: 10 mod / 40 aulas / 30h, cert valido lei 9394/96, community area aluno, suporte direto WA, apostila, 5 bonus (Coding/Render/Agentes), acesso vitalicio, 7d garantia incondicional
- **Lesson for CEXAI**: at R$97 the bar is 8/10 standard inclusions + lifetime + cert. CEXAI at 51% higher price needs to match or exceed this floor.

### Case B: BSSP IA Marketing+Vendas -- "R$997 Mid-Ticket Live"

- **URL**: https://bsspce.com.br/curso-ia-marketing-e-vendas/
- **Price**: R$997 (de R$1.490), 12x R$113.11
- **Inclusions**: 8 sessoes ao vivo Tue 19:30, mentoria 100% pratica, templates prompts exclusivos, automation models, ebook IA tendencias, cert digital, 12mo content + 6mo gravacoes, grupo exclusivo
- **Lesson for CEXAI**: Mid-ticket = founder time. CEXAI cannot afford 8 ao-vivo. **Substitute**: 4 quarterly Q&As + FAQ vivo + behind-scenes async = 90% of perceived live value at 10% of time cost.

### Case C: Academia Lendar[IA] -- "R$4.888 Premium Ecosystem"

- **URL**: https://academialendaria.ai/formacao/ (and https://hotmart.com/pt-br/marketplace/produtos/formacao-lendaria-assinatura/V91656977O)
- **Price**: R$4.888 Fundamentals (with 100% cashback into Advanced) + Recorrencia 12x parcelado on Hotmart
- **Authors**: Pedro Sobral + Conrado Adolpho (Brazilian growth/marketing celebrities)
- **Inclusions**: 3 ciclos (Basico/Pratica/Negocios), 15+ cursos modulares, comunidade ativa, eventos presenciais Brasil + mundo, Hackathon Lendario, Book Club, Legendary Women, 12 meses updates, garantia 30d 100% incondicional, multiple mentores
- **Lesson for CEXAI**: Premium ticket = ecosystem + brand stack + presence. CEXAI cannot compete here directly. But the **30d 100% refund signal** is borrowable at R$147 (extend from Hotmart minimum 7d to 14d as trust signal). See case_study_lendaria_pricing_anchor for full deep-dive.

## Recommendations (ranked)

1. **Build trilha BEFORE opening Hotmart carrinho.** 5-7 founder days. Closes 8 of 10 standard inclusions. Reduces refund risk from 25% to 8-12%. Highest ROI action.
2. **Extend Hotmart garantia 7d -> 14d.** Signal of conviction. Zero cost. Differentiator at R$147 band.
3. **Anunciar acesso vitalicio explicitamente** in sales page. Zero cost. 90% market expectation.
4. **Setup Discord ou Telegram comunidade** day 1. Zero cost. 70% market expectation.
5. **Cert PDF + apostila + glossary cheat sheet** -- batch 1-day production. Leverages existing 8F/12P/301k repo assets.
6. **8-12 quizzes auto-corretivos** -- 1 day generation per nucleus (8F-formatted).
7. **Methodology positioning** -- sales page leads with "teach typed knowledge engineering, not just tools" -- the moat narrative.
8. **Quarterly Q&A** (founder lives, 4/year) instead of weekly = sustains live signal at 10% cost.
